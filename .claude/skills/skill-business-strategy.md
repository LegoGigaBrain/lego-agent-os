# Skill: Business Strategy

## Purpose

Develop comprehensive business strategies including market positioning, competitive analysis, business models, and strategic planning. This skill provides frameworks for strategic decision-making.

## Standards Awareness

Before applying this skill, ensure familiarity with:
- `standards/signalos/transmission-framework.md`
- `standards/signalos/brand-strategy.md`

---

## Core Philosophy

> "Strategy is making choices about where to play and how to win."

Business strategy is:
- Focused choices, not comprehensive plans
- Trade-offs, not trying to do everything
- Sustainable advantage, not short-term wins
- Aligned with brand signal and values

---

## Strategy Frameworks

### 1. Playing to Win

**Where to Play:**
- Which markets/segments?
- Which geographies?
- Which channels?
- Which customer types?

**How to Win:**
- Cost leadership?
- Differentiation?
- Niche focus?
- What capabilities enable this?

```
STRATEGY CHOICE CASCADE:

1. WINNING ASPIRATION:
   What does winning look like?

2. WHERE TO PLAY:
   - Market: [Choice]
   - Segment: [Choice]
   - Geography: [Choice]
   - Channel: [Choice]

3. HOW TO WIN:
   - Competitive advantage: [Choice]
   - Differentiation: [Choice]

4. CAPABILITIES REQUIRED:
   - [Capability 1]
   - [Capability 2]

5. MANAGEMENT SYSTEMS:

```

## Quality Checklist

- [ ] Strategy involves clear choices
- [ ] Trade-offs are explicit
- [ ] Advantage is sustainable
- [ ] Strategy is aligned with brand
- [ ] Roadmap is realistic
- [ ] Metrics are defined
- [ ] Risks are addressed

---

## Anti-Patterns

- Trying to do everything
- No clear trade-offs
- Copying competitors
- Strategy without execution plan
- Ignoring market reality
- Misaligned with brand
