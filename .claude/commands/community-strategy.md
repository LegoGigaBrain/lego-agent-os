# community-strategy

You are running the **Community Strategy** workflow in LEGO Agent OS.

Goal:
Design a comprehensive community strategy including purpose, culture, member journey, rituals, and health metrics. This workflow produces a complete community operating system.

Primary Agent:
- @community-manager

Supporting Agents:
- @brand-strategist (for tribe alignment)
- @social-media-manager (for social community)
- @content-strategist (for community content)

Skills:
- Community Management
- Tribe Building
- Social Media Strategy

Standards:
- `standards/signalos/tribe-building.md`
- `standards/signalos/transmission-framework.md`
- `standards/signalos/brand-strategy.md`

---

## Steps

### 1. Clarify Community Scope

Determine what we're building:

| Scope | Description | Output |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| **New Community** | Build from scratch | Full community OS |
| **Community Audit** | Assess existing | Health report + recommendations |
| **Scale Strategy** | Grow existing | Growth playbook |
| **Culture Reset** | Fix culture issues | Culture intervention plan |

Ask:
- Does a community exist today?
- What platform(s) are we using?
- What's the community health currently?
- What resources are available?
- What are the goals for community?

### 5. Member Journey

**Goal:** Design the progression path

```
LEVEL 1: NEWCOMER (Day 1-7)

Experience:
- What they see
- What they do
- How we greet them

Goals:
- Make first post
- Complete profile
- Connect with 1+ member

Our Actions:
- Personal welcome
- Orientation guide
- Connection prompts

Success Metric:
- [% completing onboarding actions]

---

LEVEL 2: PARTICIPANT (Week 2-4)

Experience:
- Regular engagement
- Finding their place
- Learning the culture

Goals:
- Regular participation
- Answer a question OR start a discussion
- Build connections

Our Actions:
- Recognize engagement
- Invite to discussions
- Feature in spotlight

Success Metric:
- [Retention rate at 30 days]

---

LEVEL 3: CONTRIBUTOR (Month 1-3)

Experience:
- Adding value to others
- Being recognized
- Deeper belonging

Goals:
- Help other members
- Create valuable content
- Advocate externally

Our Actions:
- Public recognition
- Badge/role upgrade
- Increased access

Success Metric:
- [% who reach contributor level]

---

LEVEL 4: CHAMPION (Month 3+)

Experience:
- Core community member
- Known by others
- Leadership emerging

Goals:
- Mentor newcomers
- Lead discussions
- Co-create with brand

Our Actions:
- Champion recognition
- Ambassador invitation
- Exclusive access

Success Metric:
- [% who reach champion level]

---

LEVEL 5: LEADER (Ongoing)

Experience:
- Co-leading community
- Shaping culture
- Brand partnership

Goals:
- Moderate/lead
- Train others
- Strategic input

Our Actions:
- Official role
- Compensation/perks
- Partnership relationship

Success Metric:
- [Leader-to-member ratio]
```

### 9. Moderation & Guidelines

**Goal:** Establish community guidelines

```
COMMUNITY GUIDELINES:

- [Positive behavior 1]

- [Positive behavior 3]

DON'T:
- [Prohibited behavior 1]
- [Prohibited behavior 2]
- [Prohibited behavior 3]

CONSEQUENCES:
- First offense: [Action]
- Second offense: [Action]
- Severe violation: [Action]

---

MODERATION LEVELS:

LEVEL 1: Minor
- Issues: Off-topic, low-quality
- Action: Redirect, reminder
- Record: No

LEVEL 2: Moderate
- Issues: Repeated minor, heated
- Action: Warning, cool-down
- Record: Yes

LEVEL 3: Serious
- Issues: Harassment, hate
- Action: Remove, suspend
- Record: Yes, escalate

LEVEL 4: Severe
- Issues: Threats, illegal
- Action: Ban, report
- Record: Yes, leadership
```
