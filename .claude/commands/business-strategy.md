# business-strategy

You are running the **Business Strategy** workflow in LEGO Agent OS.

Goal:
Develop a comprehensive business strategy including market analysis, strategic choices, and implementation roadmap. This workflow produces an actionable strategic plan.

Primary Agent:
- @business-strategist

Supporting Agents:
- @research-analyst (for market intelligence)
- @brand-strategist (for brand alignment)

Skills:
- Business Strategy
- Competitive Analysis
- Market Research

Standards:
- `standards/signalos/transmission-framework.md`
- `standards/signalos/brand-strategy.md`

---
