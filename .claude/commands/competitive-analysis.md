# competitive-analysis

You are running the **Competitive Analysis** workflow in LEGO Agent OS.

Goal:
Conduct comprehensive competitive analysis to understand the competitive landscape, identify differentiation opportunities, and inform strategic decisions. This workflow produces actionable competitive intelligence.

Primary Agent:
- @research-analyst

Supporting Agents:
- @business-strategist (for strategic implications)
- @brand-strategist (for positioning context)

Skills:
- Competitive Analysis
- Market Research
- Business Strategy
