**Brand Strategy Questions & Quotes**

**Annelie Futur Framewrok:** Purpose, Mission, Vision & Goals

- What do you do?  
- Who do you do it for?  
- How do you do it?  
- Why do you do it?  
- Purpose (reason for existing beyond making money)  
- Mission  
- Vision

Brand Persona:

- Define the values your brand lives by  
- The Culture   
- Attributes & benefits  
- Behaviors  
- Tone of voice  
- Look & feel

Deeper Dive: Research (internal)

- What are you really good at?  
- What do you fee; passionate about?  
- Who is willing to pay for that?  
- Is the group of people big enough so you can make a living out of it?  
- Who are they?

Research: External Observation Research Exercise

- Who did you research?  
- Where did you look? What led you there?  
- What do they feel, think, say and do?  
- What findings surprised you?  
- What was your most important finding?

Annelie Futur Fundamental Framework:

1. Foundations  
2. Brand Personality  
3. Target Audience & research  
4. Gap Analysis  
5. Roadmap

Foundations

- Purpose  
- Vision  
- Mission  
- Goals

Brand Personality

- Values  
- Culture  
- Attributes  
- Benefits  
- Actions  
- Tone of voice  
- Look and feel

Target Audience & Research

- Who are you serving?  
- Empathy Map  
- Customer Journey Map

Gap Analysis

- Identify where you are today  
- Customer perception vs. the perception you want to create (Image vs. Identity)  
- Compatitive Analysis  
- Positioning Quadrant  
- Postioning Statement

Roadmap

- Summarize the work  
- Review insights about target audience  
- Identity gap  
- Recommendations going forward
